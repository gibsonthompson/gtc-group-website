What We Cover

Four Pillars of Cost Reduction

We target the largest expense categories in your operation, negotiating better rates across the board.

Insurance Optimization

Commercial trucking insurance is one of your largest fixed costs. We pool carriers to negotiate rates typically reserved for 100+ truck fleets.

  • Primary liability coverage
  • Cargo insurance
  • Physical damage coverage
  • General liability
  • Workers' compensation
  • Umbrella/excess policies

Fuel Programs

Fuel represents 20-30% of operating costs. Our network partnerships deliver meaningful per-gallon savings that compound across your miles.

  • National fuel card programs
  • Bulk purchasing discounts
  • Preferred pricing at major chains
  • DEF and reefer fuel savings
  • Fuel tax reporting integration
  • Route optimization for fuel costs

Maintenance Networks

Access our vetted network of service providers with pre-negotiated rates. Reduce downtime and control repair costs.

  • Preventive maintenance programs
  • Tire purchasing & retreading
  • Parts procurement discounts
  • Roadside assistance programs
  • Preferred shop networks
  • Fleet maintenance tracking

Driver Services

Recruiting and retaining drivers is expensive. We help reduce costs associated with hiring, training, and supporting your workforce.

  • Driver recruitment assistance
  • Background check services
  • Drug testing programs
  • Training & certification
  • Benefits administration
  • Compliance management
